May 2001

The synth.
 

The synth as it is at the moment. I turned it around. There is a walkway around behind it to allow access to rear of it all. Yes - five keyboards! The mostly empty rack is the ASM-1. I still need to add controls for most of the modules.

This view shows the Wurlitzer electric piano. I do have its legs, but it is a lot more convenient to have it sitting on the speakers. The Speaker box is what remains of a Welson organ I bought for $30.

Upper rack, left. VCAs, mixers, envelope generators, Psycho LFOs, filters, equalizers.

Lower rack, left. LFO, Sub oscillators, wavetables, engine sounds, flanger, phaser, vocal filter, organ interface, gate sequencer, joystick.

Tube Synth! Upper: VCA, VCF, beam modulator and VCAs, beam modulator, Hellfire clone. Lower: 2 x VCO, 2 x HFM, beam modulator, VCF.
