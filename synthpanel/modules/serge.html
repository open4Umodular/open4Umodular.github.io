Serge designs

 

Adaptations of several classic Serge modules are presented on this web site. These designs have been done with the permission and collaboration of Serge Tcherepnin himself. He also receives a royalty from each PCB sold.

Thanks to his involvement, some elements of the People's Synthesizer are again available to hobbyist synthesizer builders.
